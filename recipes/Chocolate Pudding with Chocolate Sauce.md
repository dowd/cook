## Ingredients
- 1 tsp sugar
- 5 ozs SR flour
- 1 oz chocolate powder
- 3 tbsp golden syrup
- 2 heaped tbsps choc powder
- 1 tbsp milk
- 2 large eggs
- some marg
- 4.5 ozs caster sugar
- 3 tbsp water

## Steps
 1. Cream marg and caster sugar
    [caster sugar: 4.5 ozs; marg: some]
 2. Beat eggs and add to mixture- beat.
    [eggs: 2 large]
 3. Sieve SR flour and chocolate powder into mixture. Stir gently with metal spoon. (yes it does
    make a difference!)
    [SR flour: 5 ozs; chocolate powder: 1 oz]
 4. Stir in milk
    [milk: 1 tbsp]
 5. Put mixture into a one pint greased basin.
    [–]
 6. Boil for about 1.2 hours. (Don't let it boil dry!!!!)
    [–]
 7. Put golden syrup, choc powder, water and sugar into small saucepan and gently bring to the boil.
    It should thicken.
    [choc powder: 2 heaped tbsps; golden syrup: 3 tbsp; sugar: 1 tsp; water: 3 tbsp]
 8. EAT AND ENJOY!!!!!!
    [–]
